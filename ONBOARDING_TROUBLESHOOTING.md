# Onboarding and Troubleshooting Guide

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Welcome to Scanner App by NextEleven

This guide will help you get started with the App and troubleshoot common issues. Whether you're a new user or need help with a specific problem, you'll find the information you need here.

## Getting Started

### First Launch

When you first open the App, you'll see:
1. **Launch Screen**: Brief loading screen
2. **Main Interface**: Tab bar with Library, Scan, Analytics, and Settings

### Initial Setup

**No account required!** The App works immediately:
- Start scanning receipts right away
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No login or registration needed

### Permissions

The App will request the following permissions:

**Camera Permission** (Required for scanning)
- Tap "Allow" when prompted
- If denied, enable in iOS Settings > Scanner App by NextEleven > Camera

**Photo Library Permission** (Optional, for importing existing photos)
- Allows importing receipts from your photo library
- Can be enabled later if needed

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Scan Your First Receipt

1. Tap the **Scan** tab (camera icon)
2. Tap the scan button
3. Point your camera at a receipt
4. Ensure good lighting and steady hold
5. The App will automatically detect and capture the receipt
6. Review the extracted information
7. Edit any details if needed
8. Tap "Save" to store the receipt

### Step 2: Review Your Receipt

1. Go to the **Library** tab
2. Tap on any receipt to view details
3. Review the automatically extracted information:
   - Merchant name
   - Amount
   - Date
   - Category (AI-assigned)
4. Edit any information as needed

### Step 3: Explore Categories

1. Go to **Settings** > **Categories**
2. View default receipt categories
3. View default document categories
4. Categories are automatically assigned by AI
5. You can manually change categories for any receipt

### Step 4: Check Your Analytics

1. Tap the **Analytics** tab
2. View your spending overview
3. See spending by category
4. Review monthly trends
5. Export reports (Pro feature)

## Common Tasks

### Scanning a Receipt

1. Open the Scan tab
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Hold device steady
4. Point camera at receipt
5. Wait for automatic capture
6. Review extracted data
7. Save or discard

**Tips for Best Results:**
- Use natural or bright lighting
- Keep receipt flat and in focus
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Hold device steady
- Ensure entire receipt is visible

### Editing Receipt Information

1. Open Library tab
2. Tap on a receipt
3. Tap "Edit" button
4. Modify any field:
   - Merchant name
   - Amount
   - Date
   - Category
   - Notes
5. Tap "Save"

### Changing Categories

**Automatic (AI):**
- Categories are automatically assigned
- Based on merchant name and receipt text
- All processing happens on your device

**Manual:**
1. Open a receipt
2. Tap on the category field
3. Select from available categories
4. Save changes

### Exporting Data

**PDF Export (Pro Feature):**
1. Go to Analytics tab
2. Tap export button
3. Select date range
4. Choose export options
5. Generate PDF
6. Share or save PDF

### Enabling iCloud Sync (Pro Feature)

1. Go to Settings
2. Ensure you're signed into iCloud
3. iCloud sync is automatic when enabled
4. Data syncs across all your Apple devices

## Troubleshooting

### Scanning Issues

**Problem: Camera won't open**
- **Solution:** Check camera permissions in iOS Settings > Scanner App by NextEleven > Camera
- **Solution:** Restart the App
- **Solution:** Restart your device

**Problem: Receipt not detected**
- **Solution:** Ensure good lighting
- **Solution:** Keep receipt flat and in focus
- **Solution:** Hold device steady
- **Solution:** Ensure entire receipt is visible in frame
- **Solution:** Try scanning again

**Problem: Poor text recognition**
- **Solution:** Improve lighting conditions
- **Solution:** Ensure receipt is in focus
- **Solution:** Clean camera lens
- **Solution:** Try scanning in better lighting
- **Solution:** Manually edit extracted text if needed

**Problem: Scan button doesn't work**
- **Solution:** Check if you've reached monthly scan limit (free tier)
- **Solution:** Check camera permissions
- **Solution:** Restart the App
- **Solution:** Update to latest App version

### Data Issues

**Problem: Receipts not saving**
- **Solution:** Check device storage space
- **Solution:** Restart the App
- **Solution:** Update to latest App version
- **Solution:** Contact support if issue persists

**Problem: Receipts missing**
- **Solution:** Check if iCloud sync is enabled (may be syncing)
- **Solution:** Search for receipt in Library
- **Solution:** Check date filters
- **Solution:** Verify you're signed into correct Apple ID

**Problem: Data not syncing across devices**
- **Solution:** Ensure iCloud is enabled in iOS Settings
- **Solution:** Verify same Apple ID on all devices
- **Solution:** Check iCloud Drive is enabled for the App
- **Solution:** Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
- **Solution:** Sign out and back into iCloud if needed

### App Performance

**Problem: App crashes**
- **Solution:** Force quit and restart the App
- **Solution:** Restart your device
- **Solution:** Update to latest App version
- **Solution:** Update iOS to latest version
- **Solution:** Free up device storage space
- **Solution:** Contact support with crash details

**Problem: App is slow**
- **Solution:** Close other apps to free memory
- **Solution:** Restart your device
- **Solution:** Clear old receipts if you have many
- **Solution:** Update to latest App version
- **Solution:** Check device storage space

**Problem: App won't launch**
- **Solution:** Force quit and try again
- **Solution:** Restart your device
- **Solution:** Update to latest App version
- **Solution:** Reinstall the App (data is safe with iCloud sync)

### Subscription Issues

**Problem: Premium features not working**
- **Solution:** Check subscription status in Settings
- **Solution:** Verify subscription is active in iOS Settings > Subscriptions
- **Solution:** Restore purchases (Settings > Restore Purchases)
- **Solution:** Sign out and back into App Store
- **Solution:** Contact support if issue persists

**Problem: Can't subscribe**
- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Verify App Store is working
- **Solution:** Check payment method in App Store settings
- **Solution:** Try again later
- **Solution:** Contact Apple Support for App Store issues

**Problem: Subscription not recognized**
- **Solution:** Restore purchases in Settings
- **Solution:** Sign out and back into App Store
- **Solution:** Wait a few minutes and check again
- **Solution:** Contact support with subscription details

### Category Issues

**Problem: Wrong category assigned**
- **Solution:** Manually change category for the receipt
- **Solution:** AI improves over time with usage
- **Solution:** Edit category in receipt details

**Problem: Categories missing**
- **Solution:** Go to Settings > Categories to view all categories
- **Solution:** Default categories are automatically created
- **Solution:** Contact support if categories are missing

### Export Issues

**Problem: Can't export PDF**
- **Solution:** Verify you have Pro subscription
- **Solution:** Check device storage space
- **Solution:** Try exporting a smaller date range
- **Solution:** Restart the App and try again

**Problem: Export fails**
- **Solution:** Check device storage space
- **Solution:** Try a different date range
- **Solution:** Restart the App
- **Solution:** Contact support if issue persists

## Advanced Tips

### Optimizing Scan Quality

1. **Lighting:** Use natural or bright, even lighting
2. **Angle:** Hold device perpendicular to receipt
3. **Distance:** Keep receipt in focus (not too close or far)
4. **Stability:** Hold device steady or use a flat surface
5. **Clean Lens:** Keep camera lens clean
6. **Receipt Condition:** Use clean, flat receipts when possible

### Organizing Receipts

1. **Categories:** Use categories to organize by type
2. **Search:** Use search to find specific receipts
3. **Filters:** Filter by date, category, or amount
4. **Notes:** Add notes to receipts for additional context
5. **Regular Review:** Review and organize receipts regularly

### Maximizing Free Tier

1. **Plan Scans:** Use your 10 monthly scans wisely
2. **Batch Scanning:** Scan multiple receipts in one session
3. **Review Before Saving:** Review extracted data before saving
4. **Edit Instead of Rescan:** Edit receipts instead of rescanning
5. **Upgrade When Needed:** Consider Pro for unlimited scans
