# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## General Questions

### What is Scanner App by NextEleven?

Scanner App by NextEleven is a mobile application that helps you scan, organize, and manage receipts and documents using your iPhone or iPad. The App uses AI to automatically extract information from receipts, categorize them, and provide spending insights.

### How does the App work?

1. **Scan:** Use your device's camera to scan receipts and documents
2. **Extract:** AI automatically extracts merchant name, amount, date, and other details
3. **Organize:** Receipts are automatically categorized
4. **Analyze:** View spending insights and trends in the Analytics tab
5. **Export:** Generate PDF reports for taxes or expense tracking (Pro feature)

### Is the App free?

Yes! The App offers a free tier with:
- 10 scans per month (receipts and documents combined)
- Basic AI categorization
- Local storage
- Limited analytics

Premium features require a subscription (see Subscription section).

### What devices are supported?

- iPhone (iOS 18.0 or later)
- iPad (iPadOS 18.0 or later)
- Requires camera access for scanning

### Do I need an account?

No! The App works immediately without requiring an account, login, or registration. All data is stored locally on your device.

## Scanning

### How do I scan a receipt?

1. Open the App and tap the **Scan** tab
2. Tap the scan button
3. Point your camera at the receipt
4. Ensure good lighting and hold device steady
5. The App will automatically detect and capture the receipt
6. Review the extracted information
7. Edit if needed and save

### What makes a good scan?

- **Lighting:** Use natural or bright, even lighting
- **Angle:** Hold device perpendicular to receipt
- **Focus:** Keep receipt in focus
- **Stability:** Hold device steady
- **Clean Receipt:** Use clean, flat receipts when possible

### Can I scan documents other than receipts?

Yes! The App can scan any document:
- Receipts
- Invoices
- Contracts
- IDs and licenses
- Forms
- Certificates
- Medical documents
- Legal documents
- And more

### How accurate is the text extraction?

The App uses Apple's advanced AI and NaturalLanguage frameworks for high accuracy. Accuracy depends on:
- Receipt quality and condition
- Lighting conditions
- Camera focus
- Receipt format

You can always manually edit extracted information.

### What if the App doesn't detect my receipt?

- Ensure good lighting
- Keep receipt flat and in focus
- Hold device steady
- Ensure entire receipt is visible
- Try scanning again
- Manually enter information if needed

## Subscriptions

### What's the difference between free and Pro?

**Free Tier:**
- 10 scans per month
- Basic categorization
- Local storage only
- Limited analytics

**Pro (Monthly - $4.99/month):**
- Unlimited scans
- Advanced analytics
- PDF export
- Return deadline tracking
- iCloud sync
- Priority support
- All premium features

**Pro Vault (Yearly - $29.99/year):**
- All Pro features
- AI forecasts
- Best value (saves $30/year)
- Priority support

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! New subscribers get a 7-day free trial with full access to all premium features. Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges.

### How do I subscribe?

1. Open the App and go to **Settings**
2. Tap "Scanner App by NextEleven Pro" in the Subscription section
3. Select Monthly or Yearly plan
4. Confirm purchase through Apple's App Store

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find "Scanner App by NextEleven"
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund requests must be made through Apple's App Store. We do not process refunds directly. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

### Will I lose my data if I cancel?

No! Your receipts and documents remain in the App. You'll lose access to premium features but can still view your existing data.

### Can I share my subscription with family?

Yes! The Pro Vault (Yearly) subscription supports Family Sharing for up to 5 family members. Monthly subscriptions do not support Family Sharing.

## Data and Privacy

### Is my data private?

Yes! 100% of your data is processed on your device. We never see your receipts, and they're never sent to external servers (except optional iCloud sync, which is encrypted by Apple).

### Do you sell my data?

No! We never sell or share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

### Where is my data stored?

- **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **iCloud Sync (Optional):** If enabled, data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit (encrypted by Apple)

### Can I delete my data?

Yes! You can:
- Delete individual receipts at any time
- Contact us to delete all your data
- Export your data before deletion

### Does the App work offline?

Yes! All core features work offline:
- Receipt scanning
- Categorization
- Viewing receipts
- Basic analytics

iCloud sync requires internet connection.

## Technical

### The App crashes. What should I do?

1. Force quit and restart the App
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest App version
4. Update iOS to the latest version
5. Free up device storage space
6. Contact support with crash details

### Camera won't open

1. Check camera permissions in iOS Settings > Scanner App by NextEleven > Camera
2. Restart the App
3. Restart your device

### Receipts aren't syncing

1. Ensure iCloud is enabled in iOS Settings
2. Verify you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
3. Check iCloud Drive is enabled for the App
4. Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
5. Sign out and back into iCloud if needed

### Premium features not working

1. Check subscription status in Settings
2. Verify subscription is active in iOS Settings > Subscriptions
3. Restore purchases in Settings
4. Sign out and back into App Store
5. Contact support if issue persists

### App is slow

1. Close other apps to free memory
2. Restart your device
3. Clear old receipts if you have many
4. Update to latest App version
5. Check device storage space
