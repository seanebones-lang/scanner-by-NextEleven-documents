## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I scan my first receipt?**  
A: Open the App, tap the "Scan" tab, and tap the scan button. Point your camera at a receipt and follow the on-screen instructions.

**Q: How many receipts can I scan for free?**  
A: The free tier includes 10 scans per month (receipts and documents combined).

**Q: What happens when I reach my scan limit?**  
A: You'll see an option to upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans. You can also wait until the next month for your limit to reset.

### Subscriptions

**Q: What's included in the Pro subscription?**  
A: Pro includes unlimited scans, advanced analytics, return deadline tracking, PDF export, and all premium features.

**Q: How much does Pro cost?**  
A: 
- Monthly: $4.99/month
- Yearly (Pro Vault): $29.99/year (best value, saves 50%)

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: Yes, new subscribers get a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: 
1. Open Settings on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Scanner App by NextEleven" and tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund requests must be made through Apple's App Store. We do not process refunds directly. Contact Apple Support for refund assistance.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I cancel?**  
A: No, your receipts and documents remain in the App. You'll lose access to premium features but can still view your existing data.

### Features

**Q: How does AI categorization work?**  
A: The App uses Apple Intelligence and NaturalLanguage frameworks to automatically categorize receipts based on merchant name and receipt text. All processing happens on your device - your data never leaves your phone.

**Q: Can I edit receipt information?**  
A: Yes, tap on any receipt to view and edit details like merchant name, amount, date, and category.

**Q: How do I export my receipts?**  
A: Go to the Analytics tab and tap the export button. You can generate PDF reports with all your receipts organized by category.

**Q: Does the App work offline?**  
A: Yes, all core features work offline. Receipt scanning, categorization, and viewing work without an internet connection.

**Q: Can I sync data across devices?**  
A: Yes, if you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your receipts will sync across all your Apple devices using the same Apple ID.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The App crashes when I try to scan**  
A: 
- Make sure you've granted camera permissions
- Restart the App
- Update to the latest version from the App Store
- If the issue persists, contact support with details

**Q: Receipts aren't syncing across devices**  
A: 
- Ensure iCloud is enabled in Settings
- Check that you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
- Verify iCloud Drive is enabled for the App

**Q: The scan button doesn't work**  
A: 
- Check camera permissions in iOS Settings
- Ensure you haven't reached your monthly scan limit
- Try restarting the App

**Q: Categories are incorrect**  
A: You can manually edit categories for any receipt. The AI improves over time, but manual correction is always available.

### Privacy and Security

**Q: Is my receipt data private?**  
A: Yes, 100% of your data is processed on your device. We never see your receipts, and they're never sent to external servers (except optional iCloud sync, which is encrypted by Apple).

**Q: Do you sell my data?**  
A: No, we never sell or share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I delete all my data?**  
A: Yes, you can delete individual receipts or contact us to delete all your data from the App.

### Billing

**Q: When am I charged?**  
A: 
- Monthly subscriptions: Charged monthly on the same date you subscribed
- Yearly subscriptions: Charged yearly on the anniversary of your subscription
- Free trial: No charge during the 7-day trial period

**Q: How do I see my subscription status?**  
A: Open the App, go to Settings, and check the Subscription section. You can also view it in iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

**Q: Can I switch from monthly to yearly?**  
A: Yes, cancel your monthly subscription and subscribe to the yearly plan. You'll get a prorated refund for the remaining monthly period.

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Launch
1. Force quit the App and restart
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update to the latest iOS version
4. Reinstall the App (your data is safe if iCloud sync is enabled)

### Scanning Issues
1. Check camera permissions in iOS Settings > Scanner App by NextEleven
2. Ensure good lighting when scanning
3. Hold the device steady
4. Make sure the receipt is flat and in focus

### Sync Issues
1. Check iCloud status in iOS Settings
2. Ensure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data
3. Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
4. Sign out and back into iCloud if issues persist

### Performance Issues
1. Close other apps to free up memory
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest App version
4. Clear old receipts if you have many stored
