# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Scanner App by NextEleven ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Receipt and Document Images**: Images you scan or import into the App
- **Receipt Information**: Merchant names, amounts, dates, and other details you enter or that we extract from receipts
- **User Preferences**: Settings and preferences you configure in the App

### Data Collected Automatically
- **Subscription Information**: Purchase history and subscription status (processed by Apple through StoreKit 2)
- **App Usage Data**: On-device analytics for app functionality (not shared with third parties)
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Provide receipt and document scanning services
- Enable AI-powered categorization and analysis
- Manage your subscriptions and provide premium features
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Generate analytics and insights about your spending

## Data Storage and Processing

### On-Device Processing
- **100% On-Device AI**: All AI categorization and analysis occurs entirely on your device
- **No Cloud Processing**: Receipt images and text are processed locally using Apple Intelligence and NaturalLanguage frameworks
- **No Third-Party Services**: We do not send your receipt data to any external AI services or third-party processors

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: Receipt and document data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **iCloud Sync (Optional)**: If enabled, data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted at rest using iOS security features

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your receipt information with advertisers
- Use third-party analytics services
- Track you across other apps or websites
- Share data with data brokers

### Apple Services
- **StoreKit 2**: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We receive only subscription status information.
- **CloudKit**: If enabled, data syncs through Apple's CloudKit service, which is encrypted and private.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the App
- **Delete**: Delete any receipt or document at any time
- **Export**: Export your data as PDF reports
- **Control Sync**: Enable or disable iCloud sync in Settings
- **Cancel Subscription**: Manage subscriptions through Apple's App Store settings

## Data Retention

- **Active Data**: Receipts and documents remain in the App until you delete them
- **Deleted Data**: When you delete data, it is removed from your device and iCloud (if synced)
- **Subscription Data**: Subscription information is retained as required by Apple for subscription management

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest
- **Secure Storage**: Use of iOS Keychain for sensitive information
- **No Network Transmission**: Receipt data is never transmitted over the network (except for optional CloudKit sync)
- **Regular Updates**: Security updates are included in app updates

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing in-app notification for material changes
