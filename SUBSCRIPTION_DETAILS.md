# Subscription Details and Information

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Overview

Scanner App by NextEleven offers subscription-based premium features through auto-renewable subscriptions managed by Apple's App Store. This document provides comprehensive information about our subscription plans, billing, cancellation, and refund policies.

## Subscription Plans

### Free Tier

**Features:**
- 10 scans per month (receipts and documents combined)
- Basic AI categorization
- Local storage only
- Limited analytics
- Basic receipt viewing and editing

**Price:** Free (no subscription required)

**Limitations:**
- Monthly scan limit resets on the first of each month
- No access to premium features
- No iCloud sync
- No PDF export
- No advanced analytics

### Monthly Subscription - Pro

**Product ID:** `com.mothershipai.scannerapp.monthly`

**Price:** $4.99 per month

**Features:**
- **Unlimited Scans**: No monthly limits on receipts or documents
- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed spending insights, trends, and forecasts
- **PDF Export**: Generate professional tax reports and expense summaries
- **Return Deadline Tracking**: Never miss a return deadline with smart reminders
- **iCloud Sync**: Access your receipts across all your Apple devices
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times for support inquiries
- **All Premium Features**: Access to all current and future premium features

**Billing:**
- Automatically renews monthly unless cancelled
- Charged to your Apple ID account
- Renewal occurs 24 hours before the end of the current period

**Free Trial:** 7-day free trial available for new subscribers

### Yearly Subscription - Pro Vault

**Product ID:** `com.mothershipai.scannerappannual`

**Price:** $29.99 per year

**Savings:** Save $30 per year compared to monthly subscription (50% savings)

**Features:**
- **All Pro Features**: Everything included in the monthly subscription
- **AI Forecasts**: Advanced spending predictions and trend analysis
- **Priority Support**: Highest priority for support requests
- **Best Value**: Most cost-effective option for long-term users

**Billing:**
- Automatically renews yearly unless cancelled
- Charged to your Apple ID account
- Renewal occurs 24 hours before the end of the current period

**Free Trial:** 7-day free trial available for new subscribers

## Subscription Management

### How to Subscribe

1. Open the App and navigate to Settings
2. Tap on "Scanner App by NextEleven Pro" in the Subscription section
3. Select your preferred plan (Monthly or Yearly)
4. Confirm your purchase through Apple's App Store
5. Your subscription will begin immediately (or after free trial if applicable)

### How to View Your Subscription

**In the App:**
- Go to Settings
- Check the Subscription section
- Active subscriptions will show "Pro Active" status

**In iOS Settings:**
1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Scanner App by NextEleven" to view details

### How to Cancel Your Subscription

**Important:** Cancelling your subscription does not provide a refund for the current billing period. You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

**Steps to Cancel:**
1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Scanner App by NextEleven"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
6. Confirm cancellation

**Alternative Method:**
- Visit [Apple's Subscription Management Page](https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions)
- Sign in with your Apple ID
- Find and cancel "Scanner App by NextEleven"

### Switching Between Plans

**From Monthly to Yearly:**
1. Cancel your monthly subscription
2. Subscribe to the yearly plan
3. You may receive a prorated refund for the remaining monthly period (subject to Apple's policies)

**From Yearly to Monthly:**
1. Cancel your yearly subscription
2. Subscribe to the monthly plan
3. Your yearly subscription will remain active until the end of the current period

### After Trial

## Billing and Payment

### Payment Method
- All payments are processed through Apple's App Store
- Charged to your Apple ID account
- We do not store or have access to your payment information

### Billing Cycle
- **Monthly:** Charged on the same date each month
- **Yearly:** Charged on the anniversary of your subscription date
- **Renewal:** Occurs automatically 24 hours before the end of the current period

### Price Changes
- We reserve the right to change subscription prices
- Existing subscribers will be notified 30 days in advance
- Price changes apply to new subscriptions immediately
- Existing subscriptions continue at current price until renewal

### Failed Payments
- If payment fails, Apple will attempt to charge your payment method
- You will receive notifications from Apple about failed payments
- Subscription may be suspended until payment is successful
- We are not responsible for payment processing issues

## Refunds

### Refund Policy
- **We do not process refunds directly**
- All refund requests must be made through Apple's App Store
- Apple's refund policies and procedures apply
- Refunds are subject to Apple's discretion

### How to Request a Refund
1. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find "Scanner App by NextEleven" in your purchase history
4. Select "Request a Refund"
5. Follow Apple's instructions

### Refund Eligibility
- Refunds are subject to Apple's policies
- Typically available within 90 days of purchase
- Not available for partial subscription periods
- May be available for technical issues or service problems

## Family Sharing

### Availability
- Pro Vault (Yearly) subscription supports Family Sharing
- Up to 5 family members can share the subscription
- All family members get full access to premium features

### How to Set Up
1. Ensure Family Sharing is enabled in iOS Settings
2. Add family members to your Family Sharing group
3. Family members can access premium features automatically

### Limitations
- Monthly subscriptions do not support Family Sharing
- Only the subscription owner can manage the subscription
- Family members cannot purchase additional subscriptions
